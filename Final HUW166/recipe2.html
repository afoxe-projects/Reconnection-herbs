ingredients:


Crust: 2 cups of pecan soak for 6 to 8 hours, 1 dates soak for 10 minutes. Process all in food processor until it starts to make a ball. Spray the spring pan and press nuts evenly to the bottom, freeze for about 20 minutes or until you finish making the filling.

Filling:

  • 3 cups of soaked cashew, soak for about 1 hour.
  • 1/2 cup water.
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice.
  • 3/4 coconut oil.
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  • 1/2 teaspoon Himalaya salt.
  • 3/4 cup honey.

Preparation:

Blend all the ingredients together and pour over crust level and freeze for about 3 hours. Set for about 10 minutes before you open the pan. You could top with any fruits and enjoy!
