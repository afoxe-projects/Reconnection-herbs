ingredients:


  • 2 cups macadamias, soaked 4 hours or more.
  • 1 cup pine nuts.
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice.
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast.
  • 1 yellow pepper.
  • 2 tablespoon fresh parsley.
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme.
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/2 cup water as needed.

Preparation:

Process all ingredient together adding as little of water as possilbe until a fluffy consistency is achieved.
